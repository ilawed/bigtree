Why Big Tree Learning?

The Challenge

  • Hard to learn Chinese well in Australia
  • Lack of environment to use traditional Chinese
  • Morals and values aren't commonly taught
  • Limited inspiration for children to learn

Our Solution

We create an immersive, joyful environment where children naturally absorb Chinese language and culture through:

  • Interactive storytelling and moral lessons
  • Traditional Chinese etiquette (禮) education
  • Play-based learning activities
  • Cultural immersion experiences

Our Mission

Make learning Chinese language fun, meaningful, and accessible for all ages

Our Vision

Preserve Chinese mastery through generations

Our Programs

Designed to nurture Chinese language skills from the roots up

Saturday Chinese School

Core Program
Saturdays, 9:00am–12:00pm
10 weeks per term
10-15 students per class

Curriculum:

Mandarin (Traditional), culture, listening, speaking, reading, writing

Age Groups:

3-6 years (Early learners) 7-12 years (Primary)

Levels:

  • Non-Chinese Background: Absolute Beginner → Intermediate
  • Chinese Background: Beginner → Intermediate → Advanced
Focus: Moral stories, Chinese etiquette (禮), fun & engaging learning

Extended Learning

Afternoon
Saturdays, 1-3pm & 3-5pm
10 weeks per term
5-10 students per class

Target:

Students continuing from morning class for intensive study

Focus Areas:

  • Advanced reading & writing
  • Public speaking & presentations
  • Creative writing & essays
  • Simple journaling for vocabulary building

Classic Chinese Reading

Classics
Weekdays, before/after school
1 hour sessions
4-5 students per class

Curriculum:

Chinese Classics including:

  • 論語 (Analects)
  • 孟子 (Mencius)
  • 大學 (Great Learning)
  • 中庸 (Doctrine of the Mean)
  • 易經 (Book of Changes)
  • 詩經 (Book of Songs)
  • 唐詩 (Tang Poetry)
  • 宋詞 (Song Ci Poetry)
Ages 3+ welcome
